FAQ

Common Pens Game questions

Is Pens Game an official Pittsburgh Penguins website?

No. It is an independent schedule and context page that points back to official NHL and team destinations.

How current is the game information?

The schedule is fetched from an official NHL endpoint during the site build. The page also shows the latest generation timestamp.

Does this page show live scores?

It shows completed score lines and upcoming schedule details, but the official GameCenter remains the source for live in-game status.

Can I buy tickets here?

Ticket links point to official destinations when the NHL schedule payload includes them. Otherwise use the official team schedule page.

Editorial Note

Useful because it is narrower, not noisier

The goal is not to imitate the official team site. The goal is to organize game-time information into a simpler black-and-gold reading surface, so fans can move from search intent to action in one page.

No gambling advice, no stream promises, no team branding copied into the site identity. Just a purpose-built schedule hub with original visuals.
